About VetCardioHub

VetCardioHub is dedicated to providing high-quality veterinary cardiology information through engaging videos and articles. Founded in 2025 it is committed to improving the experience of pet owners who are struggling with pets that have heart disease, or are keen to know more whether before choosing their next pet and wanting to know what breeds they should buy, or are worried about symptoms that may or may not be heart related, such as coughing. We also intend to provide relevant information to veterinary professionals seeking to improve their own understanding of the complex and rapidly evolving world of veterinary cardiology.

Inspired by a passion to improve the understanding of how to better manage pets that are affected by various conditions, but with a specific cardiology focus. By better informing and educating both pet owners and veterinarians we hope to improve the lives of pets afflicted by these conditions, all over the world.
